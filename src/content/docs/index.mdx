## Choose Your Path

Use this quick guide to decide what to read first based on your goal.

<Badge text="New Here" variant="tip" /> <Badge text="Recommended Order" />

<Steps>
1. Read `VSS Overview`

   Learn how uploads, obfuscation, library injection, version history, and deployment flow work.

2. Pick your `Client Library`

   Use `V4-HyperGuard` for current integrations, `V3` for stable legacy setups, and `Legacy v2.5` only for older compatibility.

3. Use `Web API` for automation

   Generate keys, manage blacklist/keyless entries, and fetch service analytics programmatically.
</Steps>
