Your personal account settings handle your profile, general security preferences, billing information, and access tokens. Access these settings by clicking your avatar in the top right and selecting `Settings`.

## Profile & Appearance

- **Avatar and Display Name**
  Update your profile picture and the name displayed to your contributors and users on shared pages.
- **Theme**
  Switch the dashboard interface between Light, Dark, and System default themes.
- **Email Preferences**
  Opt in or out of marketing emails, newsletters, and non-critical product updates.

## Account Security

Protect your account from unauthorized access.

<Steps>

1. **Change Password**
   Update your account password regularly.

2. **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)**
   Enable 2FA via an authenticator app (like Authy or Google Authenticator) for an essential extra layer of security. We highly recommend enabling this for all developers.

3. **Active Sessions**
   View all currently logged-in devices. Click `Revoke` to forcefully log out any unrecognized or old sessions.

</Steps>

:::caution[Recovery Codes]
When enabling 2FA, be sure to save your backup recovery codes in a secure location. If you lose access to your authenticator app and don't have recovery codes, you may lose access to your account permanently.
:::

## API Keys

API keys (Personal Access Tokens) are required to interact with the PandaAuth Web API. 

- **Creating an API Key**
  Click `Generate New Key`, give it a recognizable name (e.g., "Main Bot Script"), and define its expiration.
- **Permissions**
  Currently, API keys have full access to your account. Treat them like passwords.
- **Revoking**
  If an API key is compromised or no longer needed, click `Revoke` to invalidate it immediately.

## Billing & Subscription

Manage your PandaAuth plan and payment methods.

- **Current Plan**
  View your active platform tier, renewal date, and resource limits.
- **Upgrade/Downgrade**
  Change your subscription tier. Changes are prorated automatically.
- **Payment Methods**
  Add, remove, or update credit cards used for your subscription.
- **Invoices**
  Download previous billing statements and receipts.

## Account Deletion

If you wish to leave the platform, you can permanently delete your account.

:::caution[Permanent Action]
Deleting your account is irreversible. All your services, keys, scripts, and data will be permanently destroyed. Active subscriptions will end immediately without refund.
:::
