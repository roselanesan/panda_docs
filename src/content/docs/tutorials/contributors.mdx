Invite team members to help manage your services. Contributors receive granular permissions and can access specific parts of your service dashboard without having full ownership. Access from the sidebar → `Contributors` under your service settings.

## Permission Types

Four independent permissions can be assigned to each contributor. All permissions are per-service — a user can have different permissions on different services.

- **Manage Keys**
  Create, edit, and delete keys for the service. Includes generating keys, changing key status, resetting HWIDs, and bulk operations.

- **Manage Settings**
  Edit service configuration including general settings, security, monetization, and other service options.

- **Manage VSS**
  Access Virtual Script Storage for the service. Upload, edit, and manage scripts associated with the service. Subject to a separate VSS contributor limit based on your tier.

- **View Statistics**
  View service analytics and statistics. This is the default permission — enabled by default when inviting a contributor.

## Inviting Contributors

<Steps>

1. **Open Contributors Page**
   Navigate to your service → Settings → `Contributors`.

2. **Add Contributor**
   Click `Add Contributor`, enter the user's email address (they must already have a PandaAuth account), and select which permissions to grant.

3. **Wait for Acceptance**
   The invited user will see a pending invitation on their dashboard. They can accept or decline the invitation. Once accepted, they gain access to the service based on the assigned permissions.

</Steps>

:::note[Tier Limits]
Free (1 contributor), Basic (2), Premium (6), Enterprise (10), Special (10). VSS contributors have separate limits: Free (1), Basic (2), Premium (4), Enterprise (10), Special (10).
:::

## Managing Contributors

- **Edit Permissions**
  Click `Edit` on any contributor to change their permissions. Changes take effect immediately.

- **Remove Contributors**
  Click the delete button to remove a contributor's access. They will lose all permissions for the service immediately.

- **Contributor Status**
  - **Pending** (yellow) — Invitation sent, waiting for the user to accept
  - **Accepted** (green) — User accepted and has active access
  - **Rejected** (gray) — User declined the invitation

- **Leaving a Service**
  Contributors can leave a service from their dashboard by clicking the leave button on the shared service card. This removes their access entirely.
