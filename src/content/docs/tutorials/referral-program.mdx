The PandaAuth referral program allows you to earn credits by inviting new users to the platform. When your referred users purchase a paid plan, you earn a percentage of their payment.

## How It Works

<Steps>

1. **Get Your Link**
   Navigate to the `Referral` page in the dashboard sidebar. Here you'll find your unique referral link.

2. **Share Your Link**
   Share the link with friends, developers, or your community.

3. **Earn Credits**
   When a user signs up using your link and purchases a Premium, Enterprise, or Special tier subscription, you earn a recurring commission in PandaAuth credits.

</Steps>

## Commission Structure

- **10% Commission**
  You earn 10% of the subscription price for every user you refer who purchases a paid plan.
- **Recurring Earnings**
  As long as your referred user remains subscribed, you continue to earn the 10% commission on their renewals.

## Managing Referrals

The Referral dashboard displays all statistics related to your referral activity:

- **Total Referrals** — The number of users who have signed up using your link.
- **Active Subscriptions** — The number of referred users currently on an active paid plan.
- **Total Earnings** — The total amount of credits you've earned from all referrals.
- **Pending Payouts** — Credits currently pending processing.

### Claiming Credits

Credits earned through referrals are automatically added to your PandaAuth credit balance. You can use these credits to:

1. Upgrade your own service tiers.
2. Purchase addons (like custom domains).
3. Offset existing subscription auto-renewals.

:::note[Withdrawals]
Currently, referral earnings are credited as PandaAuth platform credits. Direct cash withdrawals are not available by default. Contact support if you have accumulated a massive amount of credits.
:::
